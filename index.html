Hassan Kamara

Hassan is currently on the second year of EY's Digital Degree Apprenticeship and sits in Technology in UKI Advisory studying towards a BSc (Hons) in Digital Innovation at ADA The National College for Digital Skills while working on engagements at EY.

He is currently working as a part of the Reporting work stream for an SAP transformation project. His main two roles in reporting have been supporting preparation for the test phase as well as being product owner for the Reporting Knowledge Portal intended for the UKI, ROI and FSO Business Operators community. Hassan has also been able to use SQL to develop proof of concepts for new reports as well as use Power BI to create a dashboard to measure testing metrics. Prior to this, Hassan was part of the Testing work stream. His biggest contribution to the role was supporting the preparation for User Acceptance Testing where he was involved in creating datasheets, resourcing testers and supporting Training. During UAT, he took part in some testing as well as supporting members of the defect triage team and individual testers. Following UAT, he was involved in creating the close out pack to gain approval from business leads.

As part of study, Hassan has already completed study in Basic Programming (Java), Data Structures and Algorithms, Testing and Automation, Database, Software Development Life Cycle, Ethics and Commercial Reasoning and Advanced Programming (Java). He has a bronze badge in Data Visualisation and wants to focus on developing skills in SQL and data warehousing within the next 12 months.

In his free time, Hassan's interests include sports such as sailing, high ropes and swimming. He also volunteers with EY Foundation's Smart Future Programmes and supports MOD cadet forces.
